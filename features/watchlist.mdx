The Watchlist feature allows you to bookmark and organize your favorite movies and TV series. Mark content you want to watch later or keep track of series you're actively following.

## Adding to Watchlist

You can add both movies and series to your watchlist:

### From Movie Details

1. Navigate to any movie's detail page
2. Click the **Add to Watchlist** button
3. The movie is instantly added to your watchlist
4. A watchlist indicator appears on the movie

### From Series Details

1. Open any series detail page
2. Click the **Add to Watchlist** button
3. The series is added to your watchlist
4. Watchlist status is visible on the series page

<Info>
Adding a series to your watchlist is required before you can enable automatic episode monitoring.
</Info>

## Viewing Your Watchlist

Access your complete watchlist from the main navigation:

1. Click **Watchlist** in the menu
2. View all your bookmarked content in one place
3. Use filters to show only movies or only series

### Watchlist Display

Each watchlist item shows:

- **Cover Art**: Movie poster or series cover image
- **Title**: Full name of the content
- **Type**: Movie or Series indicator
- **Date Added**: When you added it to your watchlist (relative time)
- **Quick Actions**: Remove button and link to details page

## Filtering Your Watchlist

Use the filter controls to organize your watchlist:

- **All**: Shows both movies and series (default)
- **Movies**: Displays only movies
- **Series**: Displays only TV series

Filters are applied instantly and preserve your selection in the URL.

## Removing from Watchlist

You can remove content from your watchlist in two ways:

### From Watchlist Page

1. Navigate to your Watchlist
2. Find the item you want to remove
3. Click the **Remove** button
4. The item is immediately removed from your list

### From Content Detail Page

1. Open the movie or series detail page
2. Click the **Remove from Watchlist** button
3. The content is removed and the button changes back to "Add to Watchlist"

<Note>
Removing a series from your watchlist while automatic episode monitoring is enabled will prompt you to disable monitoring first, or remove both simultaneously.
</Note>

## Watchlist Indicators

When browsing content, watchlist indicators help you identify bookmarked items:

- **In Watchlist Badge**: Visual indicator on content cards
- **Button State**: "Add to Watchlist" vs "Remove from Watchlist" on detail pages
- **Quick Reference**: See at a glance which content you're tracking

## Watchlist and Auto-Episodes

For TV series, the watchlist serves an important role in automatic episode monitoring:

- **Prerequisite**: Series must be in your watchlist before enabling auto-episodes
- **Linked Status**: Watchlist and monitoring are connected
- **Removal Options**: When disabling monitoring, you can choose to keep or remove from watchlist

See [Auto-Episodes](/features/auto-episodes) for details on automatic episode downloads.

## Sorting and Organization

Watchlist items are sorted by date added (newest first):

- Recently added content appears at the top
- Chronological order helps you track what you've recently bookmarked
- Filter options let you focus on specific content types

<Card title="Personal Library" icon="bookmark">
Your watchlist is personal and private. Each user has their own watchlist that's not shared with other users on the system.
</Card>

## Technical Details

### Database Structure

Watchlist entries are polymorphic:

- **User ID**: Links the watchlist item to your account
- **Watchable Type**: Identifies if it's a movie or series
- **Watchable ID**: References the specific content item
- **Created At**: Timestamp of when you added it

### Content Relationships

The watchlist system uses polymorphic relationships:

- Movies (VOD Streams) and Series both support watchlist
- Each content type maintains its own watchlist relationship
- Deletion of content automatically removes watchlist entries

<Tip>
Use your watchlist to keep track of series you're actively downloading episodes from, making it easy to return and check for new episodes.
</Tip>

## Bulk Management

While individual additions are the primary workflow, you can:

- Add multiple items one at a time while browsing
- Remove items individually from your watchlist page
- Filter to review specific types of content

## Integration with Other Features

### Download Management

Watchlist integrates seamlessly with downloads:

- Quick access to content you want to download
- Track series you're actively downloading episodes from
- Return to watchlist after starting downloads

### Search Results

Watchlist status appears in search results:

- See which search results are already in your watchlist
- Add new discoveries to your watchlist directly from search
- Filter your watchlist to find specific content quickly

### Discovery

Use your watchlist as a discovery tool:

- Bookmark content you hear about for later viewing
- Build a queue of movies and series to watch
- Track ongoing series as new episodes are released
